Scheduler - Run your Spell automatically

Configure a Scheduler​

You can enable a Scheduler on your Spell by clicking on the Scheduler button on each Spells

Once open, you can use the interface to choose the frequency, the timezone

You can even write your own cron expression to program your Spell

We'll show you, at the top of the Scheduler, the programmed interval and when the next run will occur.

You can enable and disable your scheduler and keep all your parameters by clicking on the toggle button.

Don't forget to save your changes.
