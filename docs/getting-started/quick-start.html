Quick Start

In order to make Meta-API as powerful as it is, here are some 9 steps to start using Meta API.
You can always find video tutorials and support inside our Ressource Center.

1. Read the onboard page​

The simpliest action you can do! Read the onboard page after your registration. Choose one of the 3 Open Spells or launch the Wizard to start doing some magic! You'll find more exemples on the Open Spells page.

2. Check and run a Spell for the first time​

On your Spell, check for red connectors in your Spell. It's mean that a configuration is necessary. Click on it and follow the instructions. Once everything is fine, hit the Run button to launch your Spell. The console will open automatically.

3. Obtain a valid response from a Spell​

Running your Spell will launch a request of your Spell, getting the params from the Payload. When the request is complete, it will show the data request in the Response insert.

To valid this step, the Spell should return a valid JSON object with a 2xx/3xx server response.

4. Add a Connector to a Spell​

Meta-API's mission is to make APIs communicate between them. To do so, add a Connector to a Spell by clicking the "Connector" button. A modal will appear that let you choose an API, then a Connector (API's endpoints) and will finish by a quick description of the Connector such as: params, response sample and response schema.

Click "Add a connector" and it will be added to your Spell, ready to be configure.

5. Test a Spell with a newly added Connector​

You added a new Connector? Configured it? Now it is time to test your newly Connector! Redo the step #2 and see your manipulation go live!

6. Deploy your Spell into production​

Click on the Deploy button to create a new version and promote it to production. Find here more info about deployment

To use your Spell in production, you'll need to create a version first. Go to the Spell's details and click on the "Manage versions" button and then on "Create a new version", fill up the form that appears and click "Save draft". You'll be redirected ton the Versions history.

7. Use your Spell on production​

By opening "Snippets & URLs" inside the Manage version interface, you can access to some link to test your freshly promoted Spell in a new browser tab. Try it!

8. Schedule your Spell for automatic running​

Use our Scheduler to program your Spell and make it run automatically! You can set the period you want: every hour, every days, every weeks... it's up to you.

9. Invite a friend to Meta API​

Invite a collaborator to Meta API and explore the power of the API together!
